Our Statement of Faith

Rooted in the Word. Driven by the Spirit. Focused on the Nations.

We believe the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God — the final authority for faith, life, and truth.
Scripture Reference: 2 Timothy 3:16–17 | 2 Peter 1:20–21
We believe in one eternal God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — Creator of all and Lord over all.
Scripture Reference: Deuteronomy 6:4 | Matthew 28:19 | 2 Corinthians 13:14
We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God — fully God and fully man — who lived, died, and rose again for our salvation
Scripture Reference: John 1:1–14 | 1 Corinthians 15:3–4
We believe in the person and power of the Holy Spirit, who convicts, empowers, and equips believers for godly living and global mission.
Scripture Reference: Acts 1:8 | John 14:16–17 | 1 Corinthians 12:4–11
We believe salvation is a gift of grace through faith in Jesus Christ — received through repentance and belief.
Scripture Reference: Ephesians 2:8–9 | Romans 10:9–10 | John 3:3
We believe in the one Body of Christ — the global Church — called to worship God, disciple believers, and fulfill the Great Commission.
Scripture Reference: Matthew 16:18 | Acts 2:42–47 | Ephesians 4:11–13
We believe prayer and worship are the heartbeat of revival — uniting believers with God’s purposes for the nations.
Scripture Reference: 2 Chronicles 7:14 | John 4:23–24
We believe every believer is called to be a witness of Christ, using every platform — from pulpits to media — to proclaim His Gospel to the ends of the earth.
Scripture Reference: Matthew 28:18–20 | Acts 1:8
We believe in the blessed hope — the personal, glorious return of Jesus Christ to judge the world and establish His eternal Kingdom.
Scripture Reference: Titus 2:13 | Revelation 22:12–13
We believe in a new heaven and new earth, where God dwells with His people forever in righteousness and peace.
Scripture Reference: Revelation 21:1–7

Our Confession

“We live to see Christ known, loved, and exalted among the nations — until the earth is filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord.”
Habakkuk 2:14
