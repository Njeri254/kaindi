Our Story

ANFOC Global Network was birthed out of a place of prayer and a divine burden for the nations in 2023. What began as a small circle of intercessors soon evolved into a global movement.

From the beginning, our founders were gripped by a vision they describe as “Revivolution” — a revival that matures into a revolution of righteousness, worship, and mission across generations and geographies.

One defining milestone was the Global Prayer Wave – Season 1, where believers gathered online for 234 consecutive days, praying for every nation on earth — each by name.
